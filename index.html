Bill Gates

  1. Early life

Born 1955 to a lawyer father and a mother who sat on the board at the United way. His parents always wanted him to be a lawyer. Due to his small size, Bill was bullied as a kid and often opted to stay inside. Despite this, his parents described him as having a very competitive spirit often challenging others to card games like hearts. In his early teens he became very interested in programming and made his first game, tic-tac-toe. Bill became fascinated by the world of computer programming and he and his friend Paul Allen, would frequent a local computer lab to practice their skills. They were soon banned from this lab for exploiting a bug in their computers that they used to get themselves more free time in the lab.

Bill was always a good student and he attended Harvard as a pre-law student to fulfil his parents wishes for his future.
